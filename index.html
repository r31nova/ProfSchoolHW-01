About the Band

Unprocessed is a German progressive metal band from Wiesbaden, formed in 2013. They are known for their technically sophisticated modern style of progressive metal, blending striking guitar virtuosity with catchy hooks and furious metalcore breakdowns.

The band has released five full-length albums, including their latest, "...And Everything In Between," in late 2023. Critics have praised their live performances as excellent and worth seeing.

Often compared to bands like Polyphia and Animals as Leaders, Unprocessed has carved out a unique space in the progressive metal scene with their intricate, progressive guitar riffs and melodic vocals. Their frontman, Manuel Gardner Fernandes, is recognized for his virtuosic shredding and forceful vocal delivery.
